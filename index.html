About Bluclean

We are dedicated to removing plastic pollution from water bodies using our specialized boat technology and community-driven approach.

Our Mission

Bluclean was founded with the mission to tackle the growing problem of plastic pollution in our water bodies. Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste end up in lakes, ponds, and rivers, threatening aquatic life and ecosystems.

Our innovative boat technology allows us to efficiently collect plastic waste from water surfaces without harming marine life. The collected plastic is then properly recycled or disposed of through our network of environmental organizations and dedicated volunteers.

Whether you're a private owner of a pond or a community member concerned about a public lake, Bluclean can help restore your water body to its natural state.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive solutions for plastic waste removal from various types of water bodies.

Private Pond Cleaning

We help private landowners clean their ponds from accumulated plastic waste, restoring the natural beauty and health of their water features.

Lake cleaning

Our team works with communities to clean public lakes, removing surface plastics and improving water quality for both wildlife and recreational use.

Plastic Removal

All collected plastic is sorted and sent to appropriate recycling facilities or upcycling initiatives, ensuring minimal environmental impact.
